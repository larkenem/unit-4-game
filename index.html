Crystal Collector Game

Each crystal below has a hidden value. Your task is to "mine" the crystals until you are able to match the computer's number.

Your total will be calculated by clicking each crystal. You win by matching the computer's number. You lose if you exceed the computer's number.

After each match, the crystals are assigned a new, random value and it is up to you to try and match the new random number.
